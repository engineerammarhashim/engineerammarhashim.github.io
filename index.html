Projects & Research

SDR-Based Experiments for Future 6G Frequencies

Designed SDR-based systems using HackRF One and GNU Radio Companion for transmission and reception of text, image, and audio data at higher frequency ranges.

Satellite Constellation Design for Optimal Coverage

Designed multi-satellite constellation coverage in STK for Cardiff, Perth, and Durban across S, UHF, and VHF bands, including link budget and weather impact analysis.

FPGA-Based Electrical Energy Monitoring System

Developed a Verilog-based FPGA energy monitoring system with physical voltage/current sensors, GSM SIM900A communication, cloud integration, and comparison with Arduino-Bluetooth setup.

Design of an 8-Bit Configurable Timer Peripheral

Designed a configurable timer peripheral with auto-reload register and two operating modes: one-shot and repeat mode.

Hydrogen Fuel Cell with Boost Converter

Simulated a hydrogen fuel cell system in Simulink, stepping voltage from 40V to 70V and comparing Boost and Ćuk converter output waveforms.

Dynamic Wireless Charging System for Electric Vehicles

Designed a 2 kW Level-1 wireless EV charger for a 200V peak battery at 10A and 50 kHz using inductive power transfer analysis.

Methods for Analyzing Discrete-Time Systems

Analysed systems using time-domain convolution, DFT-based frequency analysis, and Z-transform methods for output response and stability evaluation.

Spectrum Analysis of LED and Tungsten Using Spectrophotometer

Performed spectrum analysis of LED and tungsten sources using PASCO equipment, optical alignment, sensor calibration, and graphical data analysis.
